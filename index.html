The Lumara Refinery

Our recursive improvement pipeline transforms raw AI outputs into polished, high-quality content.

1

Generate

The initial solution is generated based on your prompt using your selected AI model.

2

Critique

The solution is analyzed for areas of improvement, identifying strengths and weaknesses.

3

Revise

Based on the critique, the solution is refined to address identified issues.

4

Evaluate

The improved solution is scored against the original, and the process repeats until optimal.

Technical Details

Lumara uses a sophisticated recursive improvement pipeline that leverages the power of large language models to critique and refine their own outputs. This self-improvement loop continues until the content reaches a high quality threshold or the maximum number of iterations is reached.

The system evaluates solutions based on criteria including clarity, coherence, relevance, and accuracy.
